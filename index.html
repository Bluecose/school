The Smarter Way to Learn Anything

The Summit Country Day School has been a beacon of excellence in Catholic education. Located in midtown Cincinnati, we are an independent, coeducational, college-preparatory school with a Montessori preschool and divisions through grade 12. Our mission is to develop leaders of character through superior academics and spiritual growth.
